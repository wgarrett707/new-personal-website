Seriesfarm

Code

Led a team of 5 in Founders to build an all-in-one agricultural loan and grant application platform.

Overview

Seriesfarm is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the agricultural loan and grant application process. As the Project Lead, I coordinated efforts across five team members to develop a solution that addresses the complex challenges farmers face when seeking financial assistance. The final product contained an authentication system, automated PDF filling, and a filtration page. The project was continued under a different lead for another semester after I was promoted within the organization.

Technologies
