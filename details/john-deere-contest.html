John Deere AgTech Hackathon

Presentation View Post

Pitched the inclusion of soil testing results for creating prescription maps within the John Deere Operations Center application.

Overview

Tasked with adding or fixing any feature of the Operations Center application, our team focused on the creation of prescription maps, which are geographical maps containing seed or fertilizer dispersal rate information. The current implementation lacked support for the data-heavy process of soil testing, so our solution allowed for the inclusion of soil testing results to form prescription maps.

After we placed first in the competition, we were invited to present our solution at the AgTech Summit in front of 100+ professionals in the industry.
